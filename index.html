About Us

At The Momo Xpress, we’re all about flavor, comfort, and a dash of adventure. Our specialty? Authentic Nepali dumplings—momo—made from recipes straight from the heart of Nepal.

From the first steamy bite to the last sip of our signature Jhol—a rich, spiced cold broth that hugs every dumpling—you’ll taste the tradition, love, and bold spices that make Nepali food unforgettable.

We mix old-world recipes with fast, friendly service, so you can savor the soul of Nepal—whether you’re dining in, grabbing a quick bite, or taking it to go.

Fresh. Flavorful. Unforgettable. That’s The Momo Xpress.
